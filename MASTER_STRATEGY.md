# Master Strategy

## MVP: First Launch Focus

**Starting focus:** complaints + company pages + SEO indexing.

1. **First user segment**
   - Australian consumers searching Google after a poor company experience and wanting a public complaint record.

2. **First industry focus**
   - High-search-volume consumer service businesses: trades, repairs, moving, cleaning, telecom, utilities, and retail service providers.

3. **First 3 product outcomes**
   - Consumers can submit a complaint against a company.
   - Each company has a public, indexable profile page showing complaints and response status.
   - Google can discover, crawl, and index complaint and company pages for organic acquisition.

4. **Out of scope for first launch**
   - Full dispute-resolution workflow beyond public complaint submission and company response.
   - Advanced company analytics, billing optimisation, CRM features, or enterprise tooling.
   - Native mobile apps, marketplace features, paid ads engine, and broad multi-industry expansion.
